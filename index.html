Third Edition of Symposium

In the realm of the future of food, the convergence of AI and vertical farming represents a pioneering frontier, reshaping how we grow, distribute, and consume food. Vertical farming, a practice of cultivating crops in vertically stacked layers, often within controlled environments, holds promise for addressing pressing challenges like land scarcity, water shortages, and climate change. Integrating AI into these systems further enhances efficiency, from optimizing nutrient delivery to monitoring plant health in real-time. Together, these innovations signify a paradigm shift towards sustainable, resilient, and tech-driven food systems poised to nourish our growing global population.

What is SICT Symposium?

Delve into the future of food, AI, and vertical farming with us! Our program offers a captivating exploration of how technology is revolutionizing agriculture to meet the demands of a changing world. From sustainable practices to advanced AI integration, discover the cutting-edge solutions shaping the way we grow and consume food. Join us on this transformative journey and be at the forefront of innovation in the food industry!

How do I attend?

Attention all attendees! Our program will take place at the esteemed Hall of Excellence, FUTO (Federal University of Technology, Owerri). Get ready to immerse yourself in a world of innovation and insight as we convene at this prestigious venue. Mark your calendars and prepare to join us for an unforgettable experience at the Hall of Excellence, FUTO!

What things about the theme will be discussed?

During the program, we'll delve into a range of topics at the intersection of the future of food, AI, and vertical farming. Expect discussions on innovative farming techniques such as vertical farming and hydroponics, exploring how these methods maximize efficiency and sustainability in food production. We'll also explore the role of artificial intelligence in optimizing crop growth, from predictive analytics for yield forecasting to automated monitoring of environmental conditions. Additionally, topics like food security, urban agriculture, and the impact of technology on global food systems will be thoroughly examined. Join us as we uncover the diverse facets of this dynamic theme and envision the possibilities for the future of agriculture.
